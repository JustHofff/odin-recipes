Protein Waffles

Description

These healthy, protein-packed waffles are fluffy and delicious! No waffle iron? No problem! Make them into pancakes instead. Top with berries, bananas, dark chocolate chips, or anything else you like!

Macros (Per waffle)

  • Calories: 254
  • Protein: 21g

Ingredients

  • 1 cup old-fashioned oats
  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 egg whites
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 splash pure vanilla extract, or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon, or to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat a waffle iron according to manufacturer's instructions
  2. Blend oats, cottage cheese, eggs, egg whites, honey, vanilla extract, and cinnamon in a blender until creamy
  3. Ladle batter into preheated waffle iron according to manufacturer directions and cooke the waffles until gold and crisp, about 5 minutes. Repeat until entire batch of batter is used.
